01 — Documentary Approach

An exclusive insight into the Maison's métiers.

Workplace portraits across Los Angeles and Paris brought a human dimension to the new Louis Vuitton manifesto film and accompanying social content, showing visionary crafters at work in moments of connection and collaboration.

02 — Across the Métiers

One Maison, every discipline.

Louis Vuitton's careers span far beyond the storefront: design and creation, retail, digital, manufacturing across 38 ateliers worldwide, supply chain, sustainable development, research and innovation, and more. The campaign drew on this breadth, giving each discipline a human face rather than treating "careers" as a single, generic category.

03 — Application

From archive to careers platform.

The resulting photography and film library now runs across Louis Vuitton's careers website and internal communications, offering a personal counterpoint to the Maison's product-led image.
