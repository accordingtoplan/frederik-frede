Friends
of Friends

Art Direction · Branding · Web Design · 2021

"After 11 years, a new name and a new home."

A full rebrand and website redesign for the international editorial platform — new name, new identity, new digital home for one of the world's most beloved creative communities.

Friends of Friends is an international platform dedicated to creativity and inspirational voices, born in Berlin. After 11 years and almost 700 interviews across multiple cities, the platform relaunched with a new name, new look, and new home — a simple translation from the original German that reflected its expanded international readership.

We led the complete rebrand: identity, motion system, and website redesign — building a platform that lets the stories and the people take centre stage, with a clean typographic system that scales from editorial long-reads to social media.
