Neubau Welt

2006 · Concept · Illustration · Photography

1247 vector objects. 7 spot inks.

Concept, illustration and photography for the Neubau Welt book published by Die Gestalten Verlag. 304 pages, hardcover, with CD-R, printed in 7 spot inks — a cult object of early 2000s graphic culture. Over a thousand editable vector illustrations of everyday objects, enlargeable indefinitely.
