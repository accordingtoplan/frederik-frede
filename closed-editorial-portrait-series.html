Closed

Editorial · Brand Campaign · Photography

Documentary portraits of creative practice.

An episodic documentary-style content format for fashion brand Closed, embedding seasonal collections in the daily lives of international creatives.

Closed approached the studio to develop a documentary-style content format published in episodes, naturally weaving selected fashion collections into the daily lives and neighbourhoods of leading creatives.

Authentic, in-the-moment portraits offered a multi-perspective view of style and fashion in an editorial context, supporting Closed's digital visibility and brand presence in global markets while keeping the tone intimate and human.

01 — Five Cities, Five Practices

Architecture, photography, ceramics, design.

Episodes featured architect Judith Haase in Berlin, photographer Stefan Heinrichs in the Uckermark, ceramicist Iris Roth in Milan, graphic designer Áron Filkey in New York and stylist Maryam Malakpour in Los Angeles's Laurel Canyon. Content was published across Closed's international website, its print magazine HARD COPY, its online shop and social channels, alongside the studio's own editorial platform.
