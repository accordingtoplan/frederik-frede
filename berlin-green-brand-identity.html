Berlin Green

Brand Strategy · Identity · Web Design · Art Direction

"A new narrative for conscious living."

Brand strategy, identity, and website for Berlin Green, a conscious lifestyle tech company producing sustainable, honestly designed products for the home.

Berlin Green creates new ways to bring smart lifestyle tech into people's homes, with a focus on sustainable, honest, and beautifully designed products.

The brief: rework the brand strategy to reflect future goals and highlight the community already built around it, then translate that into a new visual language across branding and website.

01 — Brand Strategy

A platform for growth, built together.

Workshops and close collaboration with the Berlin Green team identified what makes the brand unique, rethought its values, and created a platform for growth, the tools to revolutionise how lifestyle tech companies conduct and present themselves.

02 — Brand Identity

Transparency, honesty, sustainability, community.

Building on the strategy, a visual identity exploring the nature and physicality of the products. Playing with perspective in the logotype reflects sustainability and the community-focused ecosystem, while colour, typography, and pattern carry the brand's materiality and modern design approach.

03 — Website

The new identity, brought online.

A full website overhaul carrying the new brand language across every screen, from desktop to mobile, completing the shift to a coherent, values-led digital presence.
