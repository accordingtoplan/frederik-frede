Grill Royal Group

Web Design · Social Media

"One system, six personalities."

White-label web platform and social media strategy for Grill Royal Group, covering six distinct Berlin restaurants, from flagship steakhouse to Michelin-starred Thai, with one flexible system.

Grill Royal Group operates six restaurants in Berlin, each with a distinct personality, from their flagship steakhouse Grill Royal to the Michelin-starred Thai spot Kindee. One system needed to serve them all.

A nimble white-label web solution mixing design elements that can be reconfigured per restaurant. Shared navigation, individual colour palettes and branding drawn directly from each venue's character.

01 — Platform

Shared bones, distinct personalities.

Each restaurant shares the same easy-to-use navigation architecture but carries its own colour palette, typography treatment, and editorial voice, a modular system built to scale as the group grows.

02 — Social Media

A different voice for every table.

Social strategy and content production for each restaurant's individual channels, each with its own voice, developed through location-specific photography and bespoke editorial.

A group of restaurants, each with its own world.

Web platform and social media strategy for Grill Royal Group, Berlin. A flexible white-label system allowing six distinct restaurant personalities to coexist within one coherent digital framework.

Restaurants: Grill Royal, Pauly Saal, Le Petit Royal (Berlin + Frankfurt), Kindee, Einstein Unter den Linden.
