Ørgreen
Optics

Brand & Digital · 2018

"Scandinavian eyewear through a fresh lens."

Brand refresh and digital presence for Ørgreen Optics — Danish handcrafted eyewear built on colour, craft, and a distinctly Scandinavian sensibility.

Ørgreen Optics is a Danish eyewear brand built on the belief that glasses should be considered objects — handcrafted, colourful, and deeply personal. They came to us for a brand refresh and new digital home.

We positioned Ørgreen around their founding conviction: colour is craft. The digital presence reflects the brand's Scandinavian restraint while giving their extraordinary frames the spotlight they deserve.
