Sonos

Brand Activation · Editorial · Event

Listening better, told across formats.

Brand activation, editorial and event production for Sonos, spanning a trade-show pop-up and an in-home interview series.

Across two strands of work, Sonos's belief that listening out loud and listening better could create positive change was translated into both a physical brand activation and an editorial content series.

An external hub at IFA, Europe's largest consumer electronics trade show, gave press and industry an early look at the Sonos Amp, while a parallel in-home editorial series connected the brand to a community of international creatives.

01 — Sonos at Zwei Drei Raum

A press hub for IFA Berlin.

During IFA, an external hub was set up at Gallery Zwei Drei for Sonos, covering everything from conceptualisation and execution to location sourcing, styling, catering and music for the launch of the Sonos Amp and other products.

02 — In-Home Editorial Series

Sound, space and creative practice.

An in-home editorial series explored how sound and music inspire creativity, featuring BYBORRE founder Børre Akkersdijk, textile designer Nadine Goepfert, Sang Bleu founder Maxime Plescia-Bühler, photographer Vicky Grout and artist Anna Iron, each interviewed in their own space.
