Wirtschaftsclub Düsseldorf

Web Design · Art Direction

"Exclusivity and authenticity, online."

Website redesign for Wirtschaftsclub Düsseldorf, reimagining the digital home of one of Germany's top business clubs for a broader, younger audience.

WCD is the number one address for business and social connections in Düsseldorf, meeting rooms, dining, events, discussions. After a change of ownership, the brief was a comprehensive digital refresh.

The goal: reach a younger, more diverse audience while keeping existing members fully informed. A new design system built on the existing logo, with club-interior-inspired colour and new brand illustrations.

01 — Visual Identity

Built on what was already there.

The existing logo was reinterpreted for the web, a more modern design constructed around it. New brand elements, including illustrations, and a colour palette drawn directly from the club's interior.

02 — Website

Flexible enough to serve a growing club.

A set of modular content blocks handles all of the club's communication needs, memberships, room overviews, event listings, in a system designed to scale.

Where business and culture meet.

A comprehensive digital refresh for one of Germany's top business clubs, new visual identity, content system, and website built to attract a younger audience without losing the club's established character.

Part of an ongoing partnership with IAC, the club's owners.
