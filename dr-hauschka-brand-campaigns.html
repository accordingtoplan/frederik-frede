Brand Campaigns · Editorial · Event Production

Natural beauty, told through the people who live it.

Strategy, brand communication and campaign production for the organic cosmetics house, spanning editorial portraits, product campaigns and live events.

A multi-year collaboration built on shared values around creativity, social change and personal wellbeing, aimed at growing Dr. Hauschka's audience and connecting the brand with socially engaged voices.

The work spanned profile-driven brand campaigns, editorial portrait series, product campaign production and live event formats, produced across Berlin, London, Barcelona, Amsterdam and the brand's Swabian headquarters.

01 — Profile Campaign

Creatives challenging convention.

A brand campaign profiling women who challenge conventional thinking within their fields, including The Lissome Magazine creative director Dörte de Jesus and anti-war photographer Julia Leeb. Production covered custom digital storytelling, photography and film, extending into a physical event at Friends Space Berlin and a live broadcast, with editorial distribution via a global media partnership with Nowness.

02 — Let Nature In

The beauty of nature, three perspectives.

An editorial portrait series for Dr. Hauschka's Let Nature In campaign, featuring three international female creatives across Berlin, London and Barcelona, each with a distinct relationship to natural beauty. Services covered creative direction, talent selection, editorial concept, video, photography and social strategy.

03 — Summer Product Line

A campaign built around natural light.

Visual concept and production for Dr. Hauschka's Summer Product Line, shot with a diverse cast across a bespoke location set-up, focused on natural appearance, feel and atmosphere.
