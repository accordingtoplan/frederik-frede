"Building a new legacy."

A comprehensive rebrand for a family-owned California real estate developer — from logo redesign to full digital presence, bridging 60 years of history with a forward-looking visual identity.

Lewis Group of Companies is a family-owned real estate development firm with over 60 years of history in California and Nevada — specialising in mixed-use communities, residential developments, and commercial spaces. They came to us for a comprehensive rebrand to align their identity with their vision for the next chapter.

We immersed ourselves in the deep-rooted values of this family business and crafted a refreshed identity that bridges their storied past with forward-looking ambition — from logo redesign to full digital presence, business development consulting, and a new website that brings their community-driven portfolio to life.

01 — Identity

Bridging legacy and ambition.

The new visual identity reflects the warmth and community-centred nature of Lewis Group's projects — a colour palette and typography that convey both tradition and progress. The animated logo system translates seamlessly across digital and physical applications.

02 — Digital

A website as dynamic as the communities they build.

The new site highlights Lewis Group's extensive portfolio with interactive elements that let visitors explore projects and experience the company's commitment to innovation. Rich scroll animations and responsive design ensure the brand reads with the same confidence at every scale.

03 — Applications

From billboards to hard hats.

The identity system was built to live in the physical world — signage, site hoardings, stationery, and branded merchandise all carry the same confident visual language.
