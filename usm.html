Art Direction · Social Media · Content · 2017

"The interior life of inanimate objects."

A multi-channel editorial platform — Personalities by USM — showcasing how individuals relate to and style their USM furniture, with a 192-page hardcover book published by Hatje Cantz.

USM is a Swiss designer and manufacturer of high-end modular furniture — the Haller system has been a benchmark of modernist design since the 1960s. We created a multi-channel platform called Personalities by USM, showcasing how individuals relate to and style their USM products.

Working with our sister agency Friends of Friends, we developed an editorial strategy and digital roll-out: beautifully curated portraits of various Personalities with their USM furniture, paired with in-depth interviews exploring their aesthetic and views on design. The most compelling stories were gathered into a 192-page full-colour hardcover book published with Hatje Cantz.

01 — Editorial Platform

A dedicated home for people who live with USM.

The Personalities website tells the story of the USM brand through its most devoted users — creatives, designers, and makers who have built their lives and workspaces around USM furniture. Each portrait was shot by Friends of Friends photographers, pairing editorial photography with candid insight into each subject's relationship with design.

02 — Photography

Eighteen personalities. Eighteen distinct worlds.

From Berlin artists to global designers, each portrait session captured the personality of both the subject and their space — USM furniture as the constant, character as the variable.

03 — Book

192 pages. Published by Hatje Cantz.

We collated the most compelling portraits and interviews into a full-colour hardcover book — a permanent record of the Personalities project, distributed globally through the Hatje Cantz network.
