Art Direction · Web Design · 2018

"An online magazine as elegant as its readers."

Website redesign for Architectural Digest Germany — a modular, device-adaptive design that lets the content reign and gives AD's beautiful editorials room to breathe.

Architectural Digest Germany is a digital and print publication covering the best in style, architecture, interiors, and design. We worked with them on a website redesign that's classy, easy to navigate, and lets the content reign.

Our modular design works on any device — a variety of image and text formats give the online platform the feel of a real magazine, with an elegant header and refined typography that ties everything together. Effortless scrolling and subtle navigation through features, design tips, and product pages keeps readers engaged longer.
