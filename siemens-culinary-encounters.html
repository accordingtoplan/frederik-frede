Culinary Encounters — kitchens, chefs and home appliances.

An editorial film series for Siemens Home Appliances, exploring kitchen culture through the eyes of four emerging European chefs.

Culinary Encounters stepped into the kitchens of four up-and-coming chefs across Oslo, Paris, Berlin and Maastricht, examining how a new generation approaches food, kitchen culture and recipe development.

The series paired short documentary films with editorial interviews, reflecting on broader shifts in the culinary world through an intimate, home-based lens.

01 — Four Kitchens, Four Cities

Cooking as practice and place.

Each chef was filmed and interviewed in their own kitchen, with Siemens Home Appliances integrated naturally into the everyday rituals of preparation, plating and conversation.
