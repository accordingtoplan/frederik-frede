Strategy · Branding · Web Design · Motion · 2023

"Future-thinking, bold, flexible, accessible."

A complete brand transformation for a nomadic Berlin-based art foundation — new brand architecture, visual identity, animated poster system, and a Next.js website built for artist-centred storytelling.

LAS Art Foundation is a nomadic Berlin-based art foundation that collaborates with artists to create large-scale experiences both online and offline. They came to us for a complete brand transformation — new architecture, new identity, new digital home.

Our aim was to reflect the LAS approach: future-thinking, bold, flexible, accessible. We centred the artists they work with — putting their faces and voices at the heart of every platform — while maintaining concise, confident communication throughout.

01 — Identity

A visual system built for artistic flexibility.

The new brand architecture gives LAS a strong typographic foundation — bold, condensed, unapologetic — that adapts around the work of each artist they platform. Animated poster systems create motion-native assets for exhibitions and social from a single design system.

02 — Motion

Brand in motion across every channel.

From animated U-Bahn posters to social media loops and branding application reels, the identity moves as fluidly as it sits still. Every animation was designed to feel like it belongs to the art world LAS operates in — never decorative, always purposeful.

03 — Digital

A website built for performance and artistic expression.

We developed the site using Vercel, Next.js, and Sanity — prioritising both performance and content management. Interactive hover animations through the frame system maintain visual consistency with the identity throughout the browsing experience.
