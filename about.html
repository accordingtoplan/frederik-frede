Stacking skills

Built on design and creativity, scaled through agency life, platform building and studio founding. From selling NoMoreSleep to TBWA, to growing Freunde von Freunden into one of Europe's most referenced creative platforms, to running an internationally dispersed studio across Berlin and Los Angeles. The through line was never a category or a sector. Always the same question: does this earn its place, or is it just adding to the noise.

Working closely with founders, clients and collaborators who share that ambition. Across strategy, identity, and the thinking that connects them.

B Approach
Some people arrive at design through a career. For me it was always present. A quiet obsession with why some details feel right and others just exist.
That feeling never left. It just found bigger rooms to work in.
Everything gets filtered through the same three lenses: timelessness, authenticity, and usability. Not as a methodology. As a way of seeing. What earns its place in someone's life and what is just decoration without relevance. What lasts and what was only ever noise.
It shows up in the work. And everywhere else.
