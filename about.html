Where culture meets brand.

Frederik Frede is a creative director and brand strategist working at the intersection of design, culture, and commerce. Based in Berlin and Los Angeles, he helps design-led organisations find their voice and build the systems to sustain it.

Previously co-founder of MoreSleep and Freunde von Freunden, he has spent fifteen years shaping brands that live as confidently in galleries as they do in market.

C Approach
Trained as a graphic designer. Learned to think through music, production, and the culture around them.
Built businesses from zero. Agencies, platforms, communities. Before building for others became the work.
Was there when culture went digital. Helped brands understand what the internet meant for who they were, not just how they looked.
Now working where it is shifting again. Developing AI frameworks that make creative work faster, sharper, and more considered. Not less human.
The discipline doesn't change. The tools do.
