Information
Thirty years of culture,
brand, and building things
from scratch.
Started as a graphic designer in Cologne. Spent the early years designing record sleeves, concert posters, and the visual world around music — earning gold records before most agencies knew what a website was.
Founded NoMoreSleep, sold it to TBWA. Built Freunde von Freunden into one of the most referenced creative platforms in Europe — publishing two books, running a community space in Berlin, and proving that timeless content beats the news cycle every time.
Started MoreSleep in 2012 and grew it into an internationally dispersed studio, with offices in Berlin and Los Angeles, working across brand strategy, identity, digital, and content for design-led clients around the world.
Now operating independently — advising, directing, and building. Developing AI frameworks that bring rigour and creative thinking into how brands work, not just what they make. The tools are new. The discipline isn't.
