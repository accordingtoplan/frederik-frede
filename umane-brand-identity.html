Umane

Brand Identity · Design System · Motion · Digital

"Built for movement, shaped by care."

A modern performance brand for a Berlin wellness club built for ambition and driven by purpose. Identity, typography, colour, texture, and a full design system built to move.

Umane is a holistic wellness space in Berlin: exclusive locations for movement, recovery, and cutting-edge health technology, guided by top specialists and personalised programs. A modern performance system built for ambition, driven by purpose.

MoreSleep led the complete brand design: naming rationale, visual identity, typography system, colour palette, gradients, texture language, and a modular design system. The brief demanded a brand that feels intelligent, grounded, and quietly aspirational — not just a gym, a place to recharge, to heal, to transform.

01 — Logo

A wordmark built from circles.

The Umane logo is the visual cornerstone of the identity: each letterform constructed from overlapping circles, echoing the brand's core forms — the human body in motion, the dot pattern system, the gradient language. It represents who they are and what they stand for in a single, ownable mark. A lock-up system extends the wordmark across sub-brands: Umane Club, Umane Foods, Umane Health, Umane Coach.

02 — Typography

Geometric precision and human warmth.

Two typefaces define the Umane voice. Milling Duplex brings geometric precision and confident structure to headlines. Tiempos brings warmth and clarity to body copy. Together they reflect the brand's core duality: evidence-based and human-centred, structured and approachable. A system that plays with scale, weight, and contrast to breathe life into every surface.

We bring evidence to life, grounded in precision and elevated by warmth.
03 — Colour

Restrained, warm, and tactile.

Color is restrained with neutral foundations punctuated by thoughtful accents. Soft backgrounds — Healing Blue, Pale Green, Sand — create a calming atmosphere that supports mental well-being. Energetic contrasts in cyan, mint, and yellow energise and motivate. Every palette decision serves the body and the mind.

04 — Gradients & Texture

Soft circles that breathe and flow.

Three named gradients — Clarity, Balance, Focus — echo the circular forms of the logo. Fluid transitions reflect the rhythm of the human body in motion: stretching, shifting, flowing with purpose. Layered over them, a dotted texture pattern introduces tactile depth and dynamic movement, referencing the logo grid at scale. The system is modular: it moves, breathes, and organises.

05 — Design System

Every element modular and intentional.

The design system combines logo grid, marker shapes, gradient language, and dot texture into a single cohesive toolkit. Applied across web, app, social, and print: precise brand visuals generated from a simple system. Designed not to impress, but to resonate. The result is a visual identity that feels intelligent, grounded, and quietly aspirational — a direct reflection of the brand's promise: capability, calibrated.
