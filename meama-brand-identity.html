Meama

Brand Strategy · Identity

"Georgia's taste of progress."

Full brand strategy and identity for Meama, Georgia's most innovative coffee company, a national hero brand ready for international competition.

Meama is a Georgian company on a mission to make coffee easier, faster, and more enjoyable. The brief: position them as a national hero capable of taking on global competition.

A holistic scheme of strategic and creative work, brand strategy, identity, and purpose. The result positions Meama as a brand that honours Georgian heritage while facing outward.

01 — Brand Strategy

A vision made tangible.

Meama's unique vision defined. Audience segment identified. Brand narrative, values, and purpose articulated to make their ambitions relatable internally and aspirational externally.

02 — Visual Identity

Where tradition meets modernity.

An identity rooted in Georgian cultural heritage, rich colour, distinctive shapes, and a clean modern twist. A shape system born from both Georgian culture and coffee culture, where tradition meets urbanity. A full colour system extends across every blend.

A national hero, ready for the world.

Full brand strategy and visual identity for Meama, Georgia's most innovative coffee company. Positioning a Georgian brand to inspire others to take the creative spirit of the Caucasus onto the international stage.

Identity rooted in Georgian cultural heritage, with a shape system that merges tradition and modernity across the full product range.
