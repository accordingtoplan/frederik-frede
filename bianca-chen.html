"Curated Chaos"

A full brand strategy, visual identity, and digital presence for an independent Los Angeles curator — daughter of legendary tastemaker JF Chen.

Bianca Chen is an independent curator based in Los Angeles, where she's devoted her life to elevating the city's diverse art landscape, connecting its creative community, and championing emerging artists from around the world. Born and raised in LA, she grew up surrounded by art and design as the elder daughter of legendary tastemaker JF Chen.

Her curatorial approach — which we termed "Curated Chaos" — blends traditional and contemporary elements, bridging cultures, ideas, and geographies. We developed a full brand strategy, vibrant visual identity, and Webflow website from scratch, positioning her as a key influencer in the global art scene while honouring her family's deep LA legacy.
