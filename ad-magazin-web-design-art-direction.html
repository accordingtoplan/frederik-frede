"An online magazine as elegant as its readers."

Website redesign for Architectural Digest Germany: a modular, device-adaptive design that lets the content reign and gives AD's beautiful editorials room to breathe.

Architectural Digest Germany is a digital and print publication covering the best style, architecture, interiors and design from Deutschland and beyond. We worked with them on a website redesign that's classy, easy to navigate and lets the content reign.

AD's beautiful editorials and images get a chance to shine thanks to our modular design that works on any device. A variety of options and sizes for images and text give the online platform the feel of a real magazine, with an elegant header and fonts that tie everything together.
