Hospitality & Brand · 2021

"Designing for a local, global and cool mindset."

Brand strategy and hospitality design for Pernod Ricard — a programme connecting their premium spirits portfolio to the world of culture, hospitality, and contemporary living.

Pernod Ricard approached us to develop a hospitality brand strategy that could bridge their portfolio of premium spirits with the cultural contexts in which people actually drink them — bars, restaurants, hotels, events.

We developed a programme built around the idea of local, global, and cool — a mindset rather than a mandate, expressed through hospitality design, brand guidelines, and a content strategy that connected product to culture.
