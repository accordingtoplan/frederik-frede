wefox

Web Design · Art Direction · 2019

"Designing the digital future of insurance."

Website redesign and art direction for Europe's leading insurtech — making insurance simple, transparent, and human through editorial photography and clean digital design.

wefox makes finding and using insurance simpler and more transparent — but their existing website didn't communicate this. We redesigned it to match the product: clean, straightforward, and accessible.

Working with our sister agency Friends of Friends, we art-directed an editorial-style photo series that shows the empathy and warmth of wefox's actual brokers — breaking down the image of insurance as faceless and corporate. Moving type, rounded fonts, and a lively orange palette enhanced the feeling of modernity and friendliness.
